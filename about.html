About

Thoughtful front-end work with a performer's attention to detail.

I am a Front-End Developer trained through SheCodes, focused on building modern, responsive web applications, especially with React. With a background in client-facing roles, I bring a strong understanding of user needs, communication, and real-world problem solving into every project I build.

I enjoy taking ideas from concept to completion, combining thoughtful design with clean, efficient code. My experience working directly with clients of many different backgrounds has made me especially attentive to usability and the overall user experience, not just how something looks, but how it works.

Before transitioning into tech, I trained as a classical singer, which shaped my creativity, attention to detail, and ability to connect with an audience. Today, I bring that same mindset into development, creating digital experiences that are both functional and expressive.
